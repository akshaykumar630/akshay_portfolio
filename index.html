WHO I AM

My career didn't begin with a marketing degree—it began with curiosity.

While most people were scrolling through social media, I was wondering why some brands stayed in people's minds and others disappeared overnight.

Somewhere between building websites, writing captions at midnight, running Meta campaigns, and explaining to clients why marketing is more than just posting on Instagram, I found my calling.

Today, I've had the opportunity to work with fertility brands, restaurants, fashion boutiques, and growing businesses—helping them tell stories that people remember.

I've learned that businesses don't grow because of advertisements. They grow because people trust them.

And that's what I build—trust, one campaign at a time.
